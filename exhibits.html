What We Offer

The museum has a wide variety of exhibits. They include the aircraft in the Chuck Schumacher Aviation Airpark.
We also have an extensive collection of aviation and space items. Many are on display inside the museum and we have rotating exhibits that change a number of times each year. Contact the museum and ask for our Curation & Displays Director John Eckley.
