COVID-19 Update

Do to the need to provide extra protection to our visitors, hosts and members because of the Coronavirus, the museum will be closed to the public until the end of April 2020. This break will also provide time for the museum to prepare for reopening and to acquire additional hand sanitizer stations, and other precautionary measures when they become available.
If you have a tour planned at this time, you will be contacted if it is deemed necessary to cancel and reschedule your tour. You may call the museum and leave a message if you want to plan a tour starting in May 2020.

Open Cockpit Days

During the summer months we sponsor Open Cockpit Days or OCDs. These family friendly events provide many opportunities for visitors to see inside the cockpits of airplanes, visit with pilots who flew the plans, visit our displays inside the museum and displays in the Air Park.

Welcome to the Prairie Aviation Museum

We hope you enjoy the display and exhibits inside the museum and the aircraft in the Air Park. We have 8 aircraft and 3 helicopters on display. We also have one piston and two jet engines on display.
